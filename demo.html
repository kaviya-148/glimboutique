ABOUT US!

We believe that style is a way to express who you are without having a speak.Every day at glimz n glam boutique,we seek to empower our friends,family,emplyees to be more confident and to show the world their true selves.Let your fashion and style speak for ur uniqueness.

At Glimz n Glam,We are obsessive about finding those perfect boutique outfits that become the defining pieces of your wardrobe.Ever since our beginnings as a small,local womem's boutique,we have built our brand round hand-picked quality.Customers have come to love and trust.

Our drive towards comfortable, quality women's clothing began in 2010 with our owner Alisha Merrill. Alisha was a 21-year-old single mother, with a quest to support her young family without sacrificing time with her baby. With little business experience and even less money to start one, she realized that she had a great opportunity to create a business with a mission she believed in.

Starting with a tiny storefront in Lehi Utah, Alisha began by working with other consignment sellers, so that she could offer her customers a wider range of initial products. Learning from what sold well and what didn't over those first few months, she began buying her own styles and pieces for the store.

While the first year of business with no employees to help her was extremely hard, Alisha was committed to her dream of empowering women through the clothes they wear. Through talking to her customers, and seeing what wasn't available from other boutiques, she continued to search and sell eye-catching styles that weren't available anywhere else.

The first store blossomed into a second, and soon she was hiring other employees and managers who were aligned and passionate with the vision of Glimz n Glam Boutique. Two stores grew into six and then continued to grow into a nationally recognized women's boutique online.

Today Glimz n Glam Boutique's team has grown to over 50 people while offering hundreds of individually selected boutique styles online and in stores.
